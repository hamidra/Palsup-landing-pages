Our mission at Palsup is to create a comfortable environment for our pals to feel the real sense of community and help them to extend their social circles by joining like minded pals all over the world. A group of wise men once built a city in the middle of a desert with nothing but love and 10 principles. We encourage our pals to follow the following principals adapted from those 10 principles to build an inclusive community for all of our members:

Radical Inclusion: Anyone may be a part of our communities. We welcome and respect the stranger. No prerequisites exist for participation in our community, only you need to be older than 13 years to join.

Radical Self-expression: Any one is entitled to express their views and thoughts on our application as long as they are not harmful to our members or our communities. We will take action to remove the content that seems to be intended to harm a specific person, community, or society.

Communal Effort: Our community values creative cooperation and collaboration. We strive to produce, promote and protect social networks and communities that can bring our pals together. We believe there is a caring community for everyone in this world that they feel belonged. Our mission is to help our users to discover other like-minded pals to create those communities together.

Civic Responsibility: We value civil society. Community members who organize events should assume responsibility for public welfare and endeavor to communicate civic responsibilities to participants. They must also assume responsibility for conducting events in accordance with local, state and federal laws.

Participation: Our community is committed to a radically participatory ethic. We believe that transformative change, whether in the individual or in society, can occur only through the medium of deeply personal participation. We achieve being through doing. Everyone is invited to work. Everyone is invited to play. We make the world real through actions that open the heart.

Immediacy: Immediate experience is, in many ways, the most important touchstone of value in our culture. We seek to overcome barriers that stand between us and a recognition of our inner selves, the reality of those around us, participation in society, and contact with a natural world exceeding human powers. No idea can substitute for this experience. We encourage all of our members to practice mindfulness of themselves, their environments, and the other community members.
